About Our Project

See3D is designed to connect people who enjoy 3D printing with their visually impaired neighbors. Teachers, family, and low vision and blind students can request models to give new meaning to words that some students have never been able to understand. Sighted people all over Cincinnati can help this project come to life by printing requested designs and shipping them free to the blind and low vision individuals. The See3D effort has expanded beyond Cincinnati. We are reaching people in New York, Indiana, Michigan, and even across the world in Kyrgyzstan.

We hope to make See3D a go-to resource for the visually impaired everywhere.
